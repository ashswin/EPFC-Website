THE EPFC is an amateur 5-a-side football club established in 2012 that participates in the Wellington Summer Twilight Football League.

The club takes it's name from founding sponsor Elevate+ gym based in Lyall Bay who saw the immediate potential of the original squad who outlined a vision headlined by a blockbuster marquee signing who would go on to be the club's first Hall of Fame inductee. Elevate+ got out the chequebook and the rest as they say is history.

Initially created as a social side during the Rugby off-season, the club has transformed into one of the premier sides in the Sunday league often baffling opposition sides due to their unorthodox makeup of predominantly Rugby players.

The EPFC boasts one League trophy (2014) along with numerous finals appearances and can count some of the biggest names in Sunday football among their alumni.
